hello@audienz.app
We collect the stories behind pictures.
Hi, we are Pauline & Florian and we love storytelling (we really do, some of us even studied Journalism). In the over 10 years of our friendship we experienced hundreds of great stories together. The sad fact is, that most of these great memories are forgotten, overwritten by the amazing chaos of life, lost in the millions of pictures we take every day. But how was it really? Wouldn’t it be great to listen to your best stories again in the future - told by no other person than your younger self? With audienz.app we wanted to create that space, where all your life stories find a digital home - always there to be relived by you, your friends and your audienz. On audienz.app we let you collect the stories BEHIND your pictures.
Your voicestory. Your audienz.
