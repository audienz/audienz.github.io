Hi, we are Pauline & Florian and we love storytelling. In the over 10 years of our friendship, we experienced hundreds of great moments & stories together. The sad fact is, that most of these great memories are forgotten, overwritten by the amazing chaos of life, lost in the millions of pictures and texts we exchange every day. But how was it really? How did it feel? On audienz.app we let you easily collect all the memories as you would tell them. Your voicestory. Your audienz.
